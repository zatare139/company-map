Business

Tenon Medical sells the Catamaran SI Joint Fusion System, a single-implant, minimally invasive device for sacroiliac-joint fusion. Commercial-stage, single product. HQ Los Gatos, CA; ~15–30 employees. Delaware inc., IPO April 2022.

Why this is an advisory target

  • Active Nasdaq delisting fight — min-bid deficiency (Jan–Feb 2026); must regain $1.00 by ~Aug 24, 2026. Stock ~$0.22.
  • Serial reverse splits — 1:8 (Sept 2024); fresh 1:2–to–1:35 proposal in the July 2026 proxy.
  • Going-concern language in FY2024 & FY2025 10-Ks.
  • Material weakness in internal controls (segregation of duties) since IPO.
  • Chronic dilutive financing — 6+ raises in ~30 months, heavy warrant overhang; now shifting to convertible debt.
  • First-time-public CFO (Kevin Williamson).
  • ~2 analysts; mid-tier IR (MZ Group); no tier-1 bank conferences.
  • Founder-heavy board — 3 insiders on 7 seats; non-standard voting (preferred at 1.25 votes/share).
  • Single-product concentration; recent auditor change (Armanino → Haskell & White).
